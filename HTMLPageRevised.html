Types of Brew


On the left, is a picture of some drip coffee. Drip coffee is brewed with ground coffee, a filter, and some hot water. On the right is a Mocha. This drink is made with espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate. You can top it with whipped cream if you would like!

Above, you can see a guide to other drinks that baristas are well versed in! Fun fact, the Americano was created when American soldiers couldn't get drip coffee in Europe. The baristas would water down the espresso to create a more American drink.
